The Three-Generation Execution Timeline

Past audit · present diagnosis · future blueprint

Every arc of this architecture is built as a cyclical engine: it takes the raw structural data of past choices, runs it through global analytical models to diagnose our present deep-seated crises, and hands down an immediate, hyper-local protocol for the generations still to come. The first half of the series (Arcs I, II, IV, VI and VIII) is the forensic autopsy — the pre-colonial baseline of Arc VI, the financial extraction of Arc VIII, the elite boardroom failure of Arcs I and II. The second half (Arcs III, V, VII, IX and X) diagnoses why those same wounds resurface today — the algorithmic hijack of Arc X, the assembly-line burnout of Arc IX, the cultural inferiority complex of Arc VII — before resolving into a three-generation execution plan.
